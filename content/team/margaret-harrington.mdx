Margaret T. Harrington founded Justice & Partners LLP in 1999 after a decade as a partner at a leading Wall Street firm. Her vision was simple: to bring big-firm legal expertise to businesses and individuals who deserved more than an assembly-line approach to their legal matters.

## Practice Focus

Margaret's practice centers on complex commercial litigation and high-stakes corporate transactions. She has represented clients in federal and state courts across the tri-state area, argued before the Second Circuit Court of Appeals, and led the legal teams on more than $2 billion in M&A transactions over the course of her career.

Her clients include emerging technology companies, established family-owned enterprises, real estate developers, and high-net-worth individuals navigating complex business and personal legal challenges.

## Recognition

- Named to *Super Lawyers* for 15 consecutive years
- Recognized by *Best Lawyers in America* in Commercial Litigation and Corporate Law
- Recipient of the New York State Bar Association's Award for Excellence in Client Service
- Frequent speaker at the New York City Bar Association on corporate governance and M&A topics

## Beyond the Firm

Margaret serves on the board of the Legal Aid Society of New York and mentors first-generation law students through Columbia Law School's Access to Justice program. She is a lifelong New Yorker and lives in Brooklyn with her family.
