Priya S. Nair joined Justice & Partners LLP in 2010 and became a partner in 2016. Her practice spans family law, estate planning, and employment matters — areas where the legal and the deeply personal intersect. Priya is known for her calm, strategic approach to high-conflict situations and her ability to achieve results without unnecessary litigation.

## Practice Focus

Priya's family law practice covers divorce and separation, child custody and support, spousal maintenance, adoption proceedings, and prenuptial and postnuptial agreements. She represents clients in both contested and uncontested matters, and has experience in interstate and international custody disputes.

Her estate planning practice helps individuals and families protect their assets and ensure their wishes are carried out through comprehensive planning that includes wills, revocable and irrevocable trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives.

In employment law, Priya advises employers on policy development, discrimination and harassment prevention, and employment contract disputes, and represents employees in wrongful termination and hostile workplace claims.

## Notable Matters

- Obtained a significant wrongful termination settlement for a client after a discrimination investigation
